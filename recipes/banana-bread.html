Moist Banana Bread

Description

This moist banana bread is a great way to use up overripe bananas. It's easy to make and perfect for breakfast or as a snack. The rich banana flavor and tender crumb make it a family favorite.

You'll love how simple and delicious this banana bread recipe is.

Ingredients

Bread:

  • 2 to 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
